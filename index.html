About Us

The ever-increasing need for borehole water has translated into using subsurface imaging to meet the demand. Generally, the methods applied to find groundwater are very similar to those used for natural resource exploration for mineral or petroleum products. We offer an accurate, non-destructive solution to mapping the subsurface of the earth to identify potential groundwater drilling target.

Our Services

1. Today geophysical methods are used for: Mapping large geological areas in order to increase our knowledge of the Earth's structure. Locating and recovering resources such as hydrocarbons, minerals and groundwater. Mapping potential CO2 deposits in the subsurface.

In our case, we employ this technology in Identifying potential groundwater sites.

2. With our Equipments, we are able to Identify soil topography and therefore determine whether a particular site is suitable for ground water drilling or not.

3. We also Drill borehole. This is either done after we have surveyed and found water, or you you can employ our service solely for drilling boreholes.

Our services are affordable and we offer free consultation to clients.
